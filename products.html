Our Fresh Produce

Cabbage

Our farm-fresh cabbage is crisp, dense, and packed with nutrients. Ideal for salads, slaws, and cooked dishes, it offers a slightly sweet flavor and satisfying crunch. Grown without harmful chemicals, our cabbages are harvested at peak ripeness. Rich in vitamins K and C, it's a heart-healthy and immune-boosting choice. Whether shredded or stewed, it retains its texture and flavor. Store it cool and dry to keep it fresh longer. Great for home cooks and chefs alike

Lettuce

Tender and leafy, our lettuce is grown naturally for the freshest taste and texture. It’s perfect for salads, sandwiches, and wraps, offering a refreshing bite and mild flavor. We harvest early in the morning to lock in moisture and nutrients. Rich in fiber and low in calories, it supports a healthy diet. Choose from crisphead or loose-leaf varieties depending on your preference. Keep refrigerated to maintain its crunch. Your go-to green for daily freshness.
