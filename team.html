Meet the amazing team behind Vegies Hub

"Two heads are better than one"...but at Vegies hub,we believe that several intellectuals embeded with hardwork,Honesty,dedication and truthfulness makes a great foundation for progress.


Mr Batsa Ablakwa

Position - Chief Executive Officer

Emmanuel is a visionary agribusiness leader with over 15 years of experience in commercial farming and sustainable food systems. As CEO, he drives the farm’s strategic growth, partnerships, and market expansion. His leadership blends innovation with grassroots impact, empowering local communities through job creation and training. Emmanuel holds a degree in Agribusiness Management and is a frequent speaker at agricultural forums.

Ms Wendy Kipo

Position - Farm Manager

Amina has over 6 years of experience in managing agricultural projects and leading farm teams. She is responsible for overall planning, crop scheduling, and supervising all on-site operations. Her strong leadership ensures the farm runs efficiently and meets productivity targets. She also oversees compliance with safety and environmental standards.

Mr. David Samuel

Position - Lead Agronomist

David is a certified agronomist with a specialization in vegetable crops and sustainable farming. He provides expert guidance on soil health, fertilization, crop protection, and pest management. He regularly monitors field conditions and recommends improvements. His technical support helps the farm maximize yields while preserving soil quality.

Ms Rifka Ibrahim

Position - Operations Supervisor

Rifka manages the day-to-day activities on the farm, including irrigation, weeding, and harvesting. She coordinates labor schedules and ensures that tasks are carried out efficiently and on time. With her practical knowledge and strong organizational skills, she plays a key role in keeping field operations smooth. She also handles basic equipment maintenance and task reporting.

Ms Thelma Kings

Position - Marketing & Distribution Officer

Thelma leads the farm’s efforts to reach and retain buyers through effective market research and outreach. She builds partnerships with retailers, market vendors, and food businesses. Her logistics planning ensures timely delivery of fresh produce to customers. She also helps develop branding and promotional strategies for the farm's growth.

Maryam Adamu

Position - Finance & Procurement Officer

Maryam oversees all financial activities, including budgeting, procurement, payroll, and record-keeping. She ensures proper use of funds, prepares monthly reports, and maintains transparency in all transactions. With a background in agricultural finance, she ensures the farm remains cost-effective and financially sustainable. She also manages supplier relationships and purchases inputs.
